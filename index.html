Find The Cats

"You're a skinny, slobbery nerd who, at your grandma's request sets out to find all her cats"

What is the point of the game?

The essence of the game is to find various cats hidden across all kinds of imaginable and unimaginable locations. The cats you find are stored in an encyclopedia, where you can inspect each cat at any time and read its unique description. Found cats can also be equipped as pets so you can admire them. At the moment, there are 65 cats in the game, and each of them has its own rarity, which indicates how difficult it is to find. There are 6 rarities: Basic, Rare, Epic, Legendary, ???, Impossicat.

Game Description (Roblox)

You're a skinny, slobbery nerd who, at your grandma's request sets out to find all her cats (65 of them, we triple-checked) scattered across the island. Expect mysteries, secret spots, and weird funny stuff. (The developers weren't good mentally during the creation of this)
